Steven McGrath is a Russian to English translator from Iowa City, Iowa. He has over five years of experience in translation. He received a Master’s degree from Lomonosov Moscow State University, Russia’s most prominent educational institution. Steven is knowledgeable about a wide range of subjects and has welcomed translation projects of many varieties, from official documents and commercial websites to works of scholarship, journalism and literature. He is a member of the American Translators Association.
