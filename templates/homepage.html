Steven McGrath is an American Translators Association-Certified Russian to English translator from Iowa City, Iowa. He has over ten years of experience in translation. He received a master’s degree from Lomonosov Moscow State University, Russia’s most prominent educational institution. Steven is knowledgeable about a wide range of subjects and has welcomed translation projects of many varieties, from legal documents and mobile apps to works of journalism and literature, including four published books. He currently serves as assistant administrator of the ATA’s Slavic Languages Division. If you would like to discuss a project with Steven, please see his contact information .
